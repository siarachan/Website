About Me

Smiltė Marija

I am an artist from Vilnius, Lithuania who has worked with clients all over the world helping them bring concepts to life with sketches and character designs. In my designing process, I focus on both visual design and character’s story, making sure the concepts are aligned.

I am the author of several coloring books for children, featuring modern fashion designs. My goal is to create art that inspires creativity and tells a unique story through every line and color choice.
